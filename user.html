Rules

Rules of World Cup.

  • The FIFA World Cup will have 48 teams divided into 16 groups. Each team will play two matches in the group stage. The top two teams from each group will advance to the knockout stage. The knockout stage will be a single-elimination format until the final match, where the winner will be crowned the World Cup champion. In case of a tie, tiebreaker rules will be used. Matches will be 90 minutes long, with the possibility of extra time and penalty shootouts.
